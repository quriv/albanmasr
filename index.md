## About Alban Masr
Alban Masr is a dairy products marketplace app that connects customers with local dairy factories. Browse products, place orders, and get fresh dairy delivered. Factory owners can manage their inventory, receive orders, and update order status in real-time.

## App Features
- **Product Catalog**: Browse a wide selection of fresh dairy products
- **Easy Ordering**: Place orders directly from your favorite factories
- **Order Tracking**: Track your order status from confirmation to delivery
- **Factory Dashboard**: Factory owners can manage products and orders
- **Order Management**: Factories can view, accept, and update order status
- **Real-time Updates**: Get notified about your order progress
- **Cross-Platform**: Available on both iOS and Android

## User Types

### Customers (Shop Owners)
- Browse dairy products from multiple factories
- Place and track orders
- View order history
- track order updates

### Factory Owners
- Add and manage product listings
- Receive new order notifications
- View all incoming orders
- Update order status (Pending, Processing, Shipped, Delivered, Cancelled)
- Manage inventory and pricing

## Account Deletion

### App or Developer Name
Alban Masr by quriv technologies

### Steps to Request Deletion
To delete your Alban Masr account and all associated data, please email us at **support@quriv.com** with your registered email address and the subject line: **"Account Deletion Request"**

### What Data Gets Deleted
Once your identity is verified, the following data will be permanently deleted:
* Account credentials (email, phone number)
* Profile data (name, business name, business images)
* Product listings (for factory accounts)
* Order history


### What Data is Retained (If Any)
For legal, accounting, and dispute resolution purposes, some transaction metadata and order records may be retained for up to 90 days before final deletion.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I place an order?
Browse the product catalog, select items you want, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll receive a confirmation once your order is placed.

### How do factories receive orders?
Factory owners receive instant notifications when new orders are placed. They can view order details in their dashboard and update the status accordingly.

### What order statuses are available?
- **Pending**: Order received, awaiting factory confirmation
- **Processing**: Factory is preparing your order
- **Shipped**: Order is on its way
- **Delivered**: Order successfully delivered
- **Cancelled**: Order has been cancelled

### How do I track my order?
Navigate to "My Orders" in the app to see real-time status updates for all your orders.

### How do I become a factory partner?
Contact us at support@quriv.com with your business details and we'll guide you through the registration process.

### How to Report a Bug?
Please email us at support@quriv.com with:

- Screenshots if possible

## Technical Support

### App not loading properly?
1. Check your internet connection
2. Clear app cache in settings
3. Update to the latest app version
4. Restart your device

### Orders not appearing?
Please allow up to 5 minutes for orders to sync. If the issue persists, contact support.

### Can't update order status? (Factory Owners)
Verify that:
- You have a stable internet connection
- You're logged in with your factory account
- The order hasn't already been completed or cancelled

### Payment issues?
Contact our support team immediately at support@quriv.com with your order details.
