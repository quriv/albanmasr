2. DATA CATEGORIZATION AND COLLECTION METHODOLOGY
Quriv Technologies collects data to facilitate a secure B2B environment for the dairy industry.

2.1 Identity and Verification Data
To prevent market fraud and ensure the safety of food supply chains, we require:

For Consumers (Store Owners): Full legal name, a digital scan of the National Identity Card, and a photographic record of the store’s physical exterior.

For Manufacturers (Factory Owners): Full legal name, National ID of the legal representative, and a digital copy of a valid Commercial Register (السجل التجاري).

2.2 Geolocation and Technical Metadata
Precise Geolocation: Our Service utilizes GPS and cellular triangulation to determine the exact coordinates of your place of business. This is essential for the "Auto-Discovery" of locations and logistics optimization.

Device Metadata: We collect IP addresses, Media Access Control (MAC) addresses, device unique identifiers (UUID), and mobile operating system versions.

3. COMPREHENSIVE DATA UTILIZATION PROTOCOLS
Quriv Technologies processes Your information under the following operational mandates:

Account Vetting: Verifying legal documents for Factory Owners to ensure only legitimate manufacturers list products. Accounts are held in a "Pending Review" state until validated by our compliance team.

Transaction Execution: Linking products to specific brands and facilitating the order-to-invoice pipeline.

Logistical Transparency: Upon the confirmation of an order, the Consumer's GPS location and phone number are released to the Manufacturer to enable accurate delivery.

Service Analytics: We utilize anonymized data to improve product categorization (e.g., Milk, Cheese, Butter) and app performance.

4. DATA SHARING, DISCLOSURE, AND THIRD-PARTY RELATIONS
We strictly prohibit the sale or unauthorized trade of User data. Disclosure is limited to:

Market Interactivity: Data shared between Buyer and Seller to complete a commercial transaction.

Sub-Processors: We engage specialized third parties for OTP (One-Time Password) SMS delivery and cloud infrastructure (e.g., Railway or AWS). These parties are bound by Data Processing Agreements (DPA).

Legal Mandatory Disclosure: Quriv Technologies will cooperate with the Egyptian Personal Data Protection Center (PDPC) and judicial authorities if a legal warrant is presented.

5. RIGOROUS SECURITY MEASURES
Quriv Technologies employs multi-layered security protocols:

Encryption: All sensitive documents (National IDs, Commercial Registers) are encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit via TLS 1.3.

Access Control: Only authorized Quriv Technologies personnel with specific clearance can view uploaded legal documents.

Data Residency: While data may be processed in the cloud, we adhere to Egyptian regulations regarding the cross-border transfer of sensitive personal data.
